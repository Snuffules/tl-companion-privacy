Account Deletion Request - Throne and Liberty Companion

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and associated data from Throne and Liberty Companion, please:

  1. Send an email to: tl.companion.app@gmail.com
  2. Include your account email address
  3. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

Data Deletion Policy

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • User authentication data (email, profile)
  • Saved builds and build configurations
  • App preferences and settings
  • FCM notification tokens
  • Any uploaded feedback or support requests

Data Retention:

  • Most data deleted immediately upon processing
  • Backup data may be retained for up to 90 days for technical recovery purposes
  • Anonymous analytics data may be retained in aggregated form
